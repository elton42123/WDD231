Kampala Business Connect

Site Plan Document

Site Name

Kampala Business Connect

Reason for Selection: This name clearly identifies the geographical focus (Kampala, Uganda) and the primary purpose (connecting businesses). It's memorable, professional, and clearly communicates the platform's value proposition of facilitating business connections and networking within the Kampala business community.

Optional Domain Availability: kampalabusinessconnect.ug or kampalaconnect.org

Site Purpose

The Kampala Business Connect website serves as a comprehensive digital hub for local businesses in Uganda's capital city. The platform will provide:

  • Business Directory: A searchable database of local businesses with detailed profiles
  • Networking Opportunities: Event calendars and meeting facilitation for business professionals
  • Resource Center: Business development resources, market insights, and regulatory information
  • Partnership Matching: Tools to connect complementary businesses for collaboration
  • Community Engagement: Platform for business owners to share experiences and best practices

The site aims to foster economic growth by making it easier for Kampala businesses to connect, collaborate, and access valuable resources.

Scenarios

These questions represent common inquiries from our target audience:

Scenario 1: The New Entrepreneur

User: Sarah, a 28-year-old starting a boutique coffee shop in Nakasero

Question: "Where can I find reliable local suppliers for coffee beans and bakery ingredients in Kampala, and how can I connect with other restaurant owners for advice?"

Site Response: The business directory with supplier categories and the networking events section would help Sarah find suppliers and connect with experienced restaurateurs.

Scenario 2: The Established Business Owner

User: David, a 45-year-old owner of a growing IT company

Question: "How can I find potential business partners for expanding my services to new markets in East Africa, and what upcoming business networking events are happening in Kampala?"

Site Response: The partnership matching tool and comprehensive events calendar would provide David with relevant connections and networking opportunities.

Scenario 3: The International Investor

User: Maria, a venture capitalist exploring opportunities in Uganda

Question: "Which tech startups in Kampala are seeking investment, and what support services are available for foreign investors?"

Site Response: The business directory with investment-ready filters and resource center with investor information would assist Maria in her research.

Typography

The typography system combines classic serif fonts for headings with modern sans-serif for body text to create a balanced, professional appearance.

Heading Font

Georgia / Times New Roman (Serif)

Main Headings H1

Section Headings H2

Subheadings H3

Usage: All headings, navigation labels, prominent text elements

Body Font

Segoe UI / System Fonts (Sans-serif)

This is how body text will appear throughout the website. The clean, readable sans-serif font ensures excellent readability for longer content sections.

Usage: Body text, paragraphs, form labels, button text, general content
